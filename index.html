Registration Process

We require registration for all visits to ensure we provide the best possible experience for our guests.

  • Submit Registration Form: Complete our online form with your preferred date and time at least 48 hours in advance.
  • Payment: Secure your reservation with payment of the registration fee.
  • Confirmation: You'll receive a confirmation email with visit details and a unique registration code.

    • Walk-in visitors are welcome based on availability, but registered guests receive priority access and additional benefits.

    Registration Fees

    We charge a nominal registration fee to maintain our facilities and ensure quality experiences for all guests. This fee is deducted from any purchase of $50 or more during your visit.

    Cancellation Policy

    We understand that plans change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair while allowing us to accommodate other guests.

  • More than 24 hours notice: Full refund of registration fee
  • Less than 24 hours notice: 50% refund of registration fee
  • No-show: No refund issued
  • Special circumstances: Illness or emergencies handled case-by-case with documentation

    • To cancel or reschedule, please call us at least 24 hours in advance at +01 98705 43218 and provide your registration code.

    Is the registration fee per person or per group?
    The registration fee is per person, except for children under 3 who are free.
